01 Why Frontend Developer?

The intersection of visual aesthetics and logic is where I find my flow. For me, frontend development isn't just about building interfaces; it's about creating the bridge between human emotion and machine efficiency. I thrive on the immediate feedback loop of seeing a line of code transform into a tangible, interactive experience.

02 Mindset as a Developer

I approach every codebase with a curator’s eye and an engineer’s discipline. My philosophy is "Performance is User Experience." I don't just aim for visual perfection; I strive for accessibility, maintainability, and lightning-fast responsiveness. Learning is a continuous loop, not a destination.
